# Cryptolandia

Cryptolandia is an iOS app for tracking cryptocurrency prices, viewing charts, and staying updated with the latest crypto news. It's designed to be simple, fast, and reliable.

## Features

- **Real-time Price Tracking** – Stay up to date with current cryptocurrency prices
- **Interactive Charts** – View price history and trends with easy-to-read charts
- **News Feed** – Get the latest cryptocurrency news and updates
- **Clean Interface** – Simple, intuitive design that focuses on what matters

## Privacy & Support

Cryptolandia is built with privacy in mind. We don't collect personal data, and your usage stays on your device.
