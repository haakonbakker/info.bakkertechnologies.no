# Privacy Policy for Cryptolandia

_Last updated: 2025-12-04_

Cryptolandia is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.  
**In short: Cryptolandia does not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

Cryptolandia does **not** collect any of the following:

- Personal data (such as name, email, phone number, address)
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or behavior tracking
- Location data
- Financial or cryptocurrency portfolio information
- Cookies or tracking technologies

Your interactions with the app remain entirely on your device.

---

## How the App Works

Cryptolandia retrieves **public cryptocurrency price and market data** from external APIs.  
This data retrieval:

- Does **not** include any personal user information  
- Is used solely to display price charts and market updates in the app  
- Is not logged, stored, or associated with you in any way

---

## Third-Party Services

Cryptolandia does **not** use:

- Analytics services  
- Advertising networks  
- User tracking SDKs  
- Social login integrations  

No third-party service receives personal data from the app.

---

## Security

While the app does not process personal information, it uses standard iOS security features.  
If you enable Face ID or Touch ID inside the app, this authentication:

- Happens exclusively on your device  
- Is managed by iOS  
- Is never transmitted or stored externally

---

## Children’s Privacy

Cryptolandia does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.  
The app is safe for users of all ages.

---

If the app’s functionality changes in a way that affects privacy, this Privacy Policy will be updated.
